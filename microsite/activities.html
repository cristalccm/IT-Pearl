Things to Do at IBSP

From swimming to hiking, watersports and nature trails, Island Beach State Park has something for everyone!

Swimming and Watersports

Swimming:

The park boasts aproximately 1 mile of beach that is dedicated to swimming which is located in the recreation area. These areas are known as Swimming Area 1 and 2. Swimming is only permitted during the summer months when lifeguards are on duty to ensure everyone's safety. THe swimming beach is staffed with certified lifeguards trained in emergency first aid and ocean lifesaving techniques. Nearby are parking areas and pavillions with bathrooms, changing rooms, showers, and and concession and first aid stations. Swimming is only permitted when lifeguards are present.

Surfing:

The park offers opporitunities for surfing during the summer months. Surfing is permitted south of the swimming beaches. Windsurfing is also permitted in designated areas at the extreme south end of the beach.

Fishing and Hunting

Fishing:

Island Beach State Park is well known by anglers for excellent surf fishing for striped bass, summer flounder, bluefish, weakfish, and more. Bait and fishing information can be obtained at local bait and tackle shops. Fishing is permitted along the ocean beach except in the designated swimming areas. All anglers 16 and above must register for the NJ Saltwater Recreational Registry program. Access to the fishing area is open 24 hours a day.

Hunting:

Waterfowl Hunting is permitted in Sege-Island and below mean-high water areas only.

Trails and Recreation

Hiking:

Island Beach State Park has a total of 8 hiking trails. Trails lead to different parts of the bay or beach in the park. Visitors may also explore 10 miles of sandy beaches.

Biking:

There is an 8 mile main road in the park that has limited shoulder area for biking.

Horseback Riding:

Horseback riding is permitted from October 1 through April 30th. There are six miles of ocean beach in the central and southern portions of park for equestrian use and a parking area designated for horse trailers during this time. Prior reservation required. No Commercial use.
