Camping and Lodging

Although camping is not permitted in Island Beach State Park, the surrounding towns boast many campgrounds, hotels, and vactation rentals.

  • Bakers Acres Campground
  • Sea Pirate Campground
  • Atlantic Shore Pines Campground
  • Turtle Run Campground
  • Thousand Trails Chestnut Lake Campground
And many more!
