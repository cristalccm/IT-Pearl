Welcome to Bear Ridge

Step into the warmth and charm of Bear Ridge—a beautifully furnished cabin crafted for couples in search of peace, comfort, and a little magic. Nestled in the heart of upper Buffalo River country, this inviting getaway features a main floor and a cozy loft with a queen-size bed, making it the perfect retreat for two. From the moment you arrive, you'll feel right at home—and by the time you're ready to leave, you'll wish you didn't have to.

Whether you're warming up with a cup of coffee by the fire, relaxing on the comfy loveseat with a good movie, or soaking in the peaceful views just beyond the window, Bear Ridge is the kind of place where unforgettable moments are made.
