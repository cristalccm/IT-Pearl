FAQs

Got questions? We've got answers.

Each canoe rental includes paddles, life jackets (PFDs), and a mesh trash bag to help keep the river clean. Everything you need for a safe and enjoyable float is included!

No prior experience is needed! Our routes are beginner-friendly, and we'll provide basic instructions before you head out.

Children are welcome to ride along with adults. However, participants under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

At this time, we do not rent additional gear. You're welcome to bring your own dry bags, coolers, or other float gear.

Quiet hours are between the hours of 10:30 p.m. and 7:00 a.m.

We recommend bringing the following: Sunscreen, Bug spray, Towels, Dry clothes, Old tennis shoes or water shoes, Plenty to eat and drink, Life vests (two are included with each canoe rental).

Please avoid bringing the following items: Glass containers, Styrofoam coolers, No firearms (on premises or in canoes), No alcoholic beverages (prohibited by law).
